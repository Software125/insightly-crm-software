## Overview

**Insightly CRM** is a customer relationship management platform designed for managing contacts, opportunities, sales pipelines, projects, workflow automation, and business relationships.

It is especially useful for project-based businesses that need both CRM and project tracking features.

---

## Key Features

- Contact management
- Sales pipeline tracking
- Project management
- Workflow automation
- Customer relationship tracking
- Marketing automation options
- Business integrations
- Reports and dashboards
